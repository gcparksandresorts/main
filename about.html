A Brief History of Great Achievements

2014

Reuven Reyman founded Globe-City Entertainment

2016

Adventure Park Opened, the first Themed amusement park in Oras Nou!

2017

All resort properties under Globe-City Entertainment were moved to the new division: Globe-City Parks and Resorts

2018

City-Shop and the Aztech Resort Opened, finalizing the Globe-City Resort Highland.

2019

Globe-City's Oceanside Resort Opened featuring a beach-side amusement park

2021

GCPR made history with Ride the Beat: Waveriders, Minecraftias only indoor launched rollercoaster!

2023

Globe-City Parks and Resorts aquired The Diamond Center Hotel and revitalized the facility

Sustainability and Environmental Responsibility

Globe-City Parks and Resorts is an innovator in combining fun and exciting amusements with environmentally friendly and low-impact construction, energy, and resources. Staying at a Globe-City Resort means you are helping to support amazing environmental initiatives and eco-forward technologies.

Renewable Energy

Solar panels across our resorts total over 20000 square meters!

Responsible Waste and Recycling

All of our waste is sorted for recycling. Food waste across our resorts is composted and reused for Oras Nou farms!

Smart Water Systems

All of our resorts are irrigated with recycled water and water for our rides is kept on-site during referbishment to be reused.
