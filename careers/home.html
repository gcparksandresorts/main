The "Globe-City Neighborhood" Housing Program

In partnership with the Oras Nou Affordable Housing Act of 2022, Globe-City Parks and Resorts proudly provides discounted affordable housing to all of our employees working 20+ hours per week. Qualifying Cast Members can choose from 3 different apartment layouts located along Painter Ave. in Highland, Oras Nou.
